Hanover, NH — Student Assembly Elections are in full swing. On Sunday the SA Presidential and VP candidates will face off in a live debate, and on Monday voting will open for a 24 hour period starting at 5:00 p.m. To learn more about the candidates and their platforms, Anastasia Perez Ternent interviews Attiya Khan, who is running for SA President, and Sebasian Munoz-McDonald, who is running for SA Vice President on the same ticket.
