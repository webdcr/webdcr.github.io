Our Mission

WDCR News covers newsworthy events around the Dartmouth campus and Hanover as well as statewide issues and current events of interest to the Dartmouth community, in order to inform Dartmouth students, faculty and staff on news and community events.

Our Ethics

The values we strive to uphold as a student-run organization include transparency, integrity and accessibility. The working environment we will strive for will be one of collaboration, mutual respect and inclusivity.
