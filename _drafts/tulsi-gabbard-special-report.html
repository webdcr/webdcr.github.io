She spoke about the recent killing of Iranian General Qasem Soleimani, President Trump's military policies and federal spending on the military. During the question and answer period, Congresswoman Gabbard was pressed by an audience member about her interactions with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and defended her relatively high voting absence rate in the House of Representatives. WDCR News' Daniel Lam spoke with Congresswoman Gabbard about her campaign strategy in Iowa and New Hampshire, how her military experience contrasts with that of fellow Democratic presidential candidate Mayor Pete Buttigieg and her stance on Medicare for All.
