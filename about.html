Our history

WebDCR traces its roots to 1924, when members of the college's amateur radio club began broadcasting on the AM band with a federal license. The station began broadcasting as WDCR on 1340 AM in 1958 and WFRD on 99.3 FM in 1976.

Dartmouth College Radio ceased broadcasting as WDCR in 2008, and in 2021, the College sold its FM station, WFRD ("99 Rock"). Since then, WebDCR has operated as an exclusively online station.
